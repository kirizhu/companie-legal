Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

Companie ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Companie mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — email address and password, or Google account details when you sign in with Google.
  • Profile information — name, profile photo, and any other details you choose to provide.
  • Event data — events you create or attend, including event names, descriptions, dates, and locations.
  • Community data — communities you create, join, or interact with.

Device Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) — to show nearby events on a map and provide location-based features. Location data is only accessed while you use the app and is not tracked in the background.
  • Calendar (READ_CALENDAR, WRITE_CALENDAR) — to let you add events to your device calendar. We do not read or store your existing calendar data.
  • Photo library (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) — to let you select images for your profile or events. We only access photos you explicitly choose.
  • Camera — the app may include camera capability through image-picker libraries. If prompted, camera access is used solely to take photos for your profile or events.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To enable event creation, discovery, and attendance tracking.
  • To display relevant events and communities near you.
  • To improve and maintain the app.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encryption at rest and in transit. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Supabase — authentication and database.
  • Google Sign-In — authentication via your Google account.
  • Mapbox — map display and location services.
  • Expo / EAS — app build and update delivery.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the app.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

To delete your account and all associated data, open the Companie app and go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, events you created, attendance history, community memberships, and friend connections. Backup copies may be retained for up to 30 days before being fully purged from our systems.

If you are unable to access the app, you can also request account deletion by contacting us at hello@companieapp.com.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app or on this page.
